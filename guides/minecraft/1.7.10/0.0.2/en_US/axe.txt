<title>
Axe
</title>

<content>
Axes are tools used to quicken the process of collecting <guide src="minecraft:wood">wood</guide> based items. An axe can also be used as a weapon, in which role it deals a half of a heart of damage. Usage of the axe as a weapon will deal double durability damage to the axe.

A bonus chest may contain wooden and/or stone axes.

Iron and diamond axes can be bought from Weapon Smith <guide src="minecraft:villager">villagers</guide> if given <guide src="minecraft:emerald">emeralds</guide>.

Here is a list of axes:

Wood: 60 Uses
Stone: 132 Uses
Iron: 251 Uses
Golden: 33 Uses
Diamond: 1562 Uses 
</content>
